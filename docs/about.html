Briana Vecchione

Briana Vecchione is a multidisciplinary artist and researcher from Southern California and is currently based in New Jersey. Her creative practice spans mixed media collage, rhythm tap dance, personal essays, theatrical performance, and occasional poetry.

As both an artist and a scientist, she integrates her interdisciplinary skills and interests into forms of art that sit at the intersection of lived experience and intellectual inquiry, where art and analysis inform one another.

She holds a PhD in Information Science from Cornell University and currently works as a researcher at Data & Society Research Institute, where she studies AI ethics and policy for the public interest.

Briana identifies as a queer white woman of Italian American heritage and uses she/her/hers pronouns.
